About Our Temple

Our Spiritual Heritage

The Ankraha Vishnu Mandir Foundation is a revered spiritual center dedicated to Lord Vishnu, the preserver in the Hindu trinity. Our temple serves as a beacon of divine energy and spiritual guidance for the community of Palamu and beyond.

Established with the vision of preserving ancient Vedic traditions while serving the modern spiritual needs of devotees, our temple offers daily prayers, bhajans, spiritual discourses, and community service programs.

Under the divine guidance of our spiritual leaders, we strive to create an inclusive environment where people from all walks of life can explore and deepen their connection with the divine.

Our Community Services

Free Education & Tuition

We provide free coaching and tuition to underprivileged children, helping them excel in their studies and competitive exams.

Food for Needy

Regular meals and food distribution programs for poor and hungry people in our community, ensuring no one sleeps hungry.

Essential Supplies Distribution

Distribution of clothes, blankets, groceries and other essential items to poor families and individuals in need.

Children Competitions

Regular competitions and cultural programs for children to showcase their talents and develop confidence.

Recognition & Prizes

Meritorious students and talented individuals are recognized and awarded with prizes to encourage their growth.

Hunger Relief Programs

Special food distribution drives during festivals and difficult times to feed the hungry and underprivileged.

Hindu Religious Ceremonies

Regular puja, havan, katha, bhajan and all Hindu religious ceremonies as per traditional rituals.

Spiritual Education

Teaching of Bhagavad Gita, Ramayana, Mahabharata and other sacred texts to children and adults.

Festival Celebrations

Grand celebrations of all major Hindu festivals like Diwali, Holi, Janmashtami, Shivratri with community participation.
